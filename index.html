About our research

â€œWelcome to the Transport Phenomena Laboratory within the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Tufts University directed by Professor Marc Hodes. Our research is in the broad area of momentum, heat and mass transfer. Presently, we have three active projects, all funded by the National Science Foundation (NSF). They concern supercritical drying of nanomaterials in the form of aerogels, jet-impingement-based freeze drying of aerogels and enhanced dropwise condensation via the use of hybrid superhydrophobic/hydrophilic surfaces. Please see the Current Research for a complete description of these efforts. Additionally, through the Red Lotus Project, in collaboration with Professors Darren Crowdy and Demetrios Papageorgiou in the Department of Mathematics at Imperial College London, we study transport phenomena relevant adiabatic and diabatic superhydrophobic surfaces
